Welcome To Army Public School Panagarh

ARMY PUBLIC SCHOOL PANAGARH (CBSE Affiliation No. : 2480015) started its humble journey in 1974 as a Nursery School with just 10 students and then was upgraded to Garrison School in 2001. It became Army School under the aegis of AWES. (Army Welfare Education Society) on 1st Apr 2006. In Jul 2011, the School was upgraded to Army Public School, Panagarh and was accorded the Public School status.

Principal's Message

If we want to reach real peace in this world, We should start educating children. -Mahatma Gandhi

The nature of true education is reflected in one's will to learn at every stage of life. As an institution dedicated to holistic development of every child, we try our best to develop this will and support our children through different stages of life. Army Public School, Panagarh always prioritizes the experience of learning in the most sincere manner.

The past two years have been very challenging for the school with the shift from offline to online classes, implementation of pandemic restrictions, teachers adapting to new methods of teaching and the management trying to make the school resourceful despite all the difficulties. It was indeed difficult but we have accomplished much more than expected. I have the satisfaction of seeing the result of all our hard work, remarkable achievements by our students and the teachers swooning with pride and contentment dedicated for their accomplishments.

As a Principal, I know that we have a lot to do in the coming years and we are aware of our responsibilities towards this school and our nation. We wish for your support and thank you for believing in our potential.

Mr. Mukesh Kumar Parasar
Principal, APS Panagarh
